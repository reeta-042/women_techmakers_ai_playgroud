# 🤖 AI Playground: Build Your Own Image Classifier App

Welcome to the AI Playground project! 🎉

In this activity, you will:
- Train your own AI model
- Plug it into a web app
- See your model make live predictions

No prior machine learning experience required 🚀

---

## 🧠 What You’ll Learn

- What a machine learning model is
- How data is used to train AI
- How AI models are integrated into real applications

---

## 🛠️ Requirements

All you need is:
- A web browser (Chrome recommended)
- Internet connection

---

## 🎯 Step 1: Train Your Model

Go to:

👉 https://teachablemachine.withgoogle.com/

1. Choose **Image Project**
2. Create at least **2 classes** (e.g., "Phone" vs "Not Phone")
3. Add images using your webcam
4. Click **Train Model**

---

## 📦 Step 2: Export Your Model

After training:

1. Click **Export Model**
2. Choose **TensorFlow.js**

   
---

## 💻 Step 3: Run the App

1. Open the project folder
2. Open `index.html` in your browser  
   (or use Live Server in VS Code)

---

## 🔗 Step 4: Connect Your Model

1. Paste your model link into the input field in the app
2. Click **Load Model**

---

## 🎥 Step 5: Test It!

- Show objects to your webcam
- Watch your model make predictions in real-time 🤯

---

## 🧪 Try This!

- Can you confuse your model?
- What happens if your data is not balanced?
- Add more samples and retrain your model

---

## 🧩 How It Works (Simple Explanation)

- You provide **data** (images)
- The model **learns patterns**
- The app uses the model to **predict new inputs**
